Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, items must meet the following criteria:

  • Returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Items in original, unused condition
  • Original packaging and tags intact
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation)
  • No signs of damage from customer use
Note: Custom personalized items are only eligible for refund if they are defective, damaged, or significantly different from what was ordered.

Shipping Costs

Shipping cost refund policies:

  • Our Error: We cover all shipping costs (original + return)
  • Defective Items: Full shipping cost refund provided
  • Customer Preference: Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return Shipping: Customer responsible unless item is defective

Special Cases

Special refund considerations:

  • Partial Orders: If only part of your order arrives, contact us immediately
  • Damaged in Transit: Full refund plus shipping costs covered
  • Wrong Item Sent: Immediate replacement or full refund
  • Quality Issues: Full refund after quality team review
  • Delayed Delivery: Compensation or refund based on delay duration
