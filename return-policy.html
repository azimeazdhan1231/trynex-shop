Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Learn about our hassle-free return process.

Return Overview

At TryneX Lifestyle, we understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations. We offer a flexible return policy to ensure your complete satisfaction with our custom gifts and lifestyle products.

7-Day Return Window
Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery

Eligible Items

The following items are eligible for return:

  • Defective or damaged products upon arrival
  • Incorrect items sent due to our error
  • Products with significant quality issues
  • Items that don't match the description provided
Important: Custom personalized items with customer-specific designs are generally not eligible for return unless they are defective or incorrect.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom items with personal photos/text (unless defective)
  • Items damaged by customer misuse
  • Products returned after 7 days of delivery
  • Items without original packaging

Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

  • Step 1: Contact us within 7 days of delivery via WhatsApp or email
  • Step 2: Provide your order ID and reason for return
  • Step 3: Wait for return authorization and instructions
  • Step 4: Pack the item securely in original packaging
  • Step 5: Send the item to our return address (provided upon authorization)
  • Step 6: Receive confirmation and refund within 3-5 business days

Refund Information

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 3-5 business days.

Free Return Shipping
We cover return shipping costs for defective or incorrect items

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, color, or design, please return the original item and place a new order. This ensures faster processing and better service.

Special Circumstances

If you receive a damaged package or if there's an issue during delivery, please take photos of the packaging and item, then contact us immediately. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

Need Help with Returns?

Our customer service team is here to help you with any return-related questions.
